Puerto Rican Cuisine

Breakfast

1. Mofongo Breakfast Bowl
  • Carbs: Mashed green plantains
  • Protein: Scrambled eggs with turkey sausage
  • Fats: Avocado slices
  • Extras: Topped with sautéed onions and a drizzle of olive oil
2. Avena con Frutas Tropicales
  • Carbs: Creamy oatmeal with cinnamon
  • Protein: A scoop of Greek yogurt
  • Fats: Shredded coconut and chia seeds
  • Extras: Topped with fresh mango and pineapple chunks
3. Tostadas Boricuas
  • Carbs: Whole-grain toast with a light spread of guava paste
  • Protein: Grilled ham and a poached egg
  • Fats: Butter or ghee on toast
  • Extras: Side of mixed greens with olive oil vinaigrette

Lunch

1. Pollo Guisado Plate
  • Carbs: White or brown rice with pigeon peas (arroz con gandules)
  • Protein: Stewed chicken (pollo guisado)
  • Fats: Sliced avocado
  • Extras: Side of sweet plantains (maduros)
2. Pernil and Sweet Potato Mash
  • Carbs: Mashed sweet potatoes with cinnamon
  • Protein: Slow-roasted pork shoulder (pernil)
  • Fats: Crispy pork skin garnish
  • Extras: Garlic sautéed green beans
3. Chillo al Ajillo Plate
  • Carbs: Coconut rice with pigeon peas
  • Protein: Red snapper in garlic sauce
  • Fats: Olive oil in the garlic sauce
  • Extras: Side of grilled asparagus

Dinner

1. Bistec Encebollado Plate
  • Carbs: Mashed yuca or boiled green bananas
  • Protein: Steak with caramelized onions
  • Fats: Drizzle of olive oil over yuca
  • Extras: Side salad with tomatoes and avocado
2. Asopao de Camarones
  • Carbs: Puerto Rican rice soup with tomatoes
  • Protein: Shrimp
  • Fats: Garnished with olive oil
  • Extras: Tostones (fried green plantains)
3. Vegetarian Pastelón
  • Carbs: Layers of sweet plantains
  • Protein: Lentils and black beans
  • Fats: Cheddar cheese and olive oil
  • Extras: Side of sautéed spinach with garlic

Snacks and Add-ons

  • Alcapurrias: Green banana and yucca fritters stuffed with ground turkey (carbs, protein, fats).
  • Empanadillas: Whole-wheat turnovers filled with chicken or vegetables.
  • Flan de Coco: Coconut flan (dessert portioned for balanced fats and carbs).
  • Smoothies: Tropical fruit smoothies with almond milk and whey protein.

Customization Options

  • Substitute proteins (chicken, pork, fish, tofu).
  • Choose your carb (white rice, brown rice, sweet potatoes, or yuca).
  • Low-fat or high-protein variations available upon request.

Weekly Meal Program

  1. Balanced Macros: All meals tailored to include 40% carbs, 30% protein, and 30% fats.
  2. Portion Sizes: Small (300-400 cal), Medium (500-600 cal), and Large (700-800 cal).
  3. Packages:
    • 5-day plan: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner (15 meals).
    • 7-day plan: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner (21 meals).
  4. Delivery Options: Freshly made meals delivered twice weekly.
